YOUR NAME

2016 | Fantasy/Romance | 1h 50min



Directed by: Makoto Shinkai

Your Name is a 2016 Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai, celebrated for its breathtaking visuals and emotional depth. The story follows two teenagers, Mitsuha Miyamizu and Taki Tachibana, who are inexplicably connected by a mysterious phenomenon. Mitsuha, a high school girl living in the rural town of Itomori, dreams of escaping her quiet life and experiencing the energy of Tokyo. Taki, a high school boy in Tokyo, juggles school, a part-time job, and his aspirations of becoming an architect.
